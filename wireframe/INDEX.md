- Key metrics per stage

- Form completion rates
- Lead quality indicators

5. Report monthly performance to leadership
6. Adjust messaging based on bottlenecks

- Awareness: 10,000 page views, ~40-50% bounce
- Interest: ~3-4 clicks/session, 2-3 min avg
- Consideration: ~5-6 sections, 4-5 min avg
- Decision: 50-100 form opens/day
- Conversion: ~5-15 leads/day (~1.5-2%)
